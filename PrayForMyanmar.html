About Josiah Mawia

My name is Josiah Mawia, born and raised in Myanmar (Burma). I was adopted by missionary evangelist parents who faithfully taught me the Scriptures from childhood.

I placed my faith in Christ at a young age and was mentored by my father, who recognized and nurtured God's call to ministry in my life.

Today, I travel throughout Myanmar's tribal regions, sharing the Gospel in villages that have never heard the name of Jesus, training local believers, and planting churches among unreached people groups.
